Tailwind Config Color Spectrum

If you use Tailwind CSS and you have a lot of custom colors in your tailwind.config.js file, it can get confusing.

With this tool, you can paste the contents of your entire config file into a textarea and see what colors and color classes you're using. This will help avoid duplicates or near duplicates, and also help you pick a good custom suffix to place a new color properly within the light-to-dark gradient.

Click on a color bar to copy its class suffix to your clipboard.
